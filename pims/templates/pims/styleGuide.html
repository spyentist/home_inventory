Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share information about Ryan and Nichole Price as they are getting married.

Have you heard that Nichole and Ryan(or I) are getting married? Check out the website that Ryan(or I) made to figure out when you can come for our receptions or find out some things about either of us and how we met. Also you can find our registries and other fun things.

Audience

The main people who will be looking at this website are going to be looking for information about Nichole, Ryan, the receptions, or how they met. They will also be entertained by the images and stories that are shared on the site which are written by both Ryan and Nichole. People will be able to encounter this site via a QR code that will be located on the wedding invitation and via social media posts that will attract attention.

Our Story

How We Met June 29th of 2019 is when the whole story began. Ryan was invited by his roommate Josh to go bowling with some friends for a fun night out and Nichole just happened to be a member of this group. As Ryan always says, he instantly wanted to get to know and become friends with Nichole, (not to mention Ryan thought she was quite pretty.) After bowling, we decided to go play some games in the park, and while up at BYU-Idaho, always includes a round of sardines. On Ryan’s turn to hide, Nichole was the lucky one to find him first, and of course, Ryan hid in the bushes and Nichole had to hide in them with him. This was our first time talking and being alone that night and of course, Nichole’s first words to him were “Hi, my name is Nichole.” (Talk about awkward!) Finding out that night we learned we both loved video games and decided to start playing together any chance we could. This started the beginning of the end of a great semester. Nichole ended up being at Ryan’s apartment every day since that day we met, just hanging out with all his roommates and FHE sisters. Most days, she was there longer than Ryan was! Many a movie was watched, games were played, food was cooked, and friends were happy. Feelings were also starting, but with Nichole planning on serving a mission, (heck, she was almost done with her papers when she met Ryan, and had them submitted by the end of the semester) Nichole shot down Ryan many times, but good thing Ryan is persistent and knew that chasing after this “out-of-control” girl was eventually going to be worth it. A last hoorah came at the end of the semester where Ryan and Josh graciously accepted to give Nichole a ride to Utah to catch a flight back home to Michigan. We hugged and said our final goodbyes for almost 2 years- or so we thought. As soon as Nichole got home, she realized how much Ryan meant to her and how much she really did like him and could not deny it anymore. Calls were made and usually lasted for hours every day. We laughed at the idea of Ryan coming to visit Nichole, but never really took it seriously until Brittany (Nichole’s sister) decided to call him and ask him to be Nichole's date to her friends' wedding. Being freaked that this could actually happen, Nichole said the ticket could not be more than $300. The first ticket that he found was $299. Heavenly Father sometimes has a sense of humor. Ryan came to visit the second week of August, and the first day he was there, we made our relationship official, and by the end of that fantastic week together, we decided through lots of prayer that marriage was likely in our future. Nichole decided to put her mission papers away for good because a new, wonderful, and exciting path was placed in her way - and it is so much sweeter. Ryan and Nichole were officially engaged on October 13th, 2019, and will be sealed for time and all eternity on January 3rd, 2020.
